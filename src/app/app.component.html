What is this site?

Ever had trouble remembering numbers? Worry not, you are only a human, and Drillmajor is here to help. This simple game will teach you the mnemonic major system, and allow you to hone your skills and track your progress anywhere.

How to play?

Simply click the big red button when you remember what word is associated with the number. Click the back arrow to give up and to not store your time.

What is the major system?

As abstract symbols, numbers themselves are very difficult for the human mind to store. However, if you associated a more concrete object for each number, remembering even long strings of integers would be easy. For example, think of the number 007 and you might instantly imagine James Bond. You can think of this text a week later, remember James Bond, and effortlessly also recall the number I used in this example: 007. The major system itself works by associating specific sounds to each digit between 0 and 9, so that you can systematically turn any number into words and any word into a number. It is quite powerful: For example 34729401215721110 translates to American Presidential Candidates. I wonder which one is easier to remember?

The sounds for each digit are the following:

  • 0: S, Z or C sound
  • 1: T or D sound
  • 2: N sound
  • 3: M sound
  • 4: R sound
  • 5: L sound
  • 6: J, Sh, soft Ch or soft G sound
  • 7: K, C sound
  • 8: F or V sound
  • 9: B or P sound
